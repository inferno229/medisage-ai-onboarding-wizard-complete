# Ayurvedic Remedies for Digestion

Ayurveda suggests several natural remedies for improving digestion (Agni):

1. **Ginger (Adrak):** Consuming a small piece of fresh ginger with a pinch of salt before meals can stimulate digestive enzymes.
2. **Cumin, Coriander, and Fennel (CCF Tea):** Drinking tea made from these three seeds helps detoxify and balance the digestive system.
3. **Warm Water:** Sipping warm water throughout the day helps clear Ama (toxins) from the body.
4. **Triphala:** A combination of three fruits that acts as a gentle colon cleanser.

Avoid cold drinks during meals as they "quench" the digestive fire.
