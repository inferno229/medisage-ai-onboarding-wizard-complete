# Yoga for Stress and Anxiety

Yoga is a powerful tool for calming the nervous system.

## Key Asanas (Poses)
1. **Child's Pose (Balasana):** Gently stretches the back and calms the brain.
2. **Corpse Pose (Savasana):** Essential for deep relaxation and reducing stress.
3. **Cat-Cow (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana):** Improves spinal flexibility and releases tension.
4. **Legs Up the Wall (Viparita Karani):** Excellent for grounding and reducing anxiety.

## Pranayama (Breathing)
1. **Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing):** Balances the two hemispheres of the brain.
2. **Bhramari (Humming Bee Breath):** Instantly calms a racing mind.
3. **Box Breathing:** Used by elite forces for focus and stress control.

Always practice on an empty stomach and listen to your body's limits.
